How Lottery of People Works
Step 1

Game designer creates a game and posts it for Sponsorship

Step 2

Distributors signs up retailers

Step 5

Customer selects a game to play and makes payment in crypto or cash to the retailer

Step 6

Everyone involved in the sale, get the commission and the winner gets prize
